HAWAIIAN SALAD
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
8 Ounces pineapple tidbits 7 Cups salad greens, torn 1 Cup cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 Cup mayonnaise 1 Tablespoon sugar
Drain pineapple, reserving 1 tablespoon juice. In a large bowl, combine greens, pienapple and cheese.
In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sguar and reserved pineapple juice; mix well. Pour over salad; toss to coat. Serve immediately.
