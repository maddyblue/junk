FROSTY EGGNOG
Recipe By : The Argus Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Cup orange juice 1 Cup eggnog 1 packet orange frost 1 1/2 Cups cracked ice
Combine ingredients in blender container. Blend at high speed for 30 seconds or until smooth.
