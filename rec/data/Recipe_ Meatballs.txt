MEATBALLS
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Pound ground beef, not lean 3 eggs
1/2 Cup romano cheese, grated 1 Cup bread crumbs 1 clove garlic, chopped fine
parsley
Mix together with hands, roll into balls. Bake 325 degrees 10-15 minutes. I make small one. Also add 1/2 pound mild Italian sausage links cut up into 1 inch pieces. Add to spagehtti sauce and simmer 1 hour or more with meatballs
