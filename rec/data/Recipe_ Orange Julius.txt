ORANGE JULIUS
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories :
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Cup Water 1 Cup Milk 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1/2 Can Orange Concentrate 1/4 Cup Sugar 10 Ice Cubes
Take all ingredients (only 3 ice cubes) and pulse them in a blender until you cannot hear ice. Add the rest of ice cubes 3 at a time and blend until you cannot hear cubes.
