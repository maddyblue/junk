JOHNNY CAKES
Recipe By : Serving Size : 15 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Cups `cornmeal 1 Cup flour 2 Teaspoons soda 1 Teaspoon salt 2 Tablespoons molasses 3 Cups buttermilk 2 eggs, beaten
Sift dry ingredients. Slowly stir in molasses and buttermilk. Add beaten eggs and beat 2 minutes.
Place in shallow baking pan that is well greased.
Bake 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
