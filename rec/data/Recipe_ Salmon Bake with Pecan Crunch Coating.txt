SALMON BAKE WITH PECAN CRUNCH COATING
Recipe By : Denver Post Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Fish
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Tablespoons Dijon mustard 2 Tablespoons butter, melted 4 Teaspoons honey
1/4 Cup bread crumbs, fresh 1/4 Cup pecan or walnuts, chopped 2 Teaspoons parsley, chopped 4 salmon fillet, thawed, 5 ounces
lemon wedges
Mix together mustard, margarine and honey in small bowl; set aside.
Mix together bread crumbs, pecans and parsley in a samll bowl; set aside.
Season each salmon fillet with salt and pepper. Place on a lightly greased baking sheet or broiling pan. Brush each fillet with mustard-honey mixture. Pat top of each fillet with pecan-bread crumb mixture. Bake in a 450 degree over for 10 minutes or until salmon just begins to flake easily when tested with a fork. Serve with lemon wedges.
