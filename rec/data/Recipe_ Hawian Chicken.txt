HAWIAN CHICKEN
Recipe By : Denise Wood Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
4 chicken breats, cooked cut into cubes 15 1/2 Ounces Pineapple,Canned In Juice,Chunks
1/2 Cup honey 2 Tablespoons corn starch
1/2 Cup vinegar, red wine 1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce 1 Chicken Bouillon Cube,Unprepared 1 green pepper, cut up 1 onion, cut into strips
1/4 Teaspoon ginger
Sauce: Reserve juice of pineapple and add water to make 1 1/2 cups liquid. Add honey, starch, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger and bouillon cube to liquid. Bring to boil and boil 2 minutes stirring constantly. Put chicken and pineapple in casserole (2 quart). Pour sauce over chicken and pineapple.
Bake uncovered 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Serve with rice.
