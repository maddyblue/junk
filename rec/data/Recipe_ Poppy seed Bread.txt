POPPY SEED BREAD
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
GLAZE 3/4 Cup sugar 1/4 Cup orange juice 1/2 Teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 Teaspoon almond extract 1/2 Teaspoon butter flavoring
BREAD 3 Cups flour, sifted 2 1/2 Cups sugar 1 1/2 Teaspoons salt 1 1/2 Teaspoons baking powder 3 eggs 1 1/2 Cups milk 1 1/8 Cups vegetable oil 1 1/2 Teaspoons poppy seeds 1 1/2 Teaspoons vanilla extract 1 1/2 Teaspoons almond extract 1 1/2 Teaspoons butter flavoring
Grease and lightly flour two 9 x 5 inch loaf pans. Preheat oven to 350. Mix all bread ingredients together, then beat for 2 minutes. Pour into pans and bake for 1 hour.
Mix glaze ingredients together in a small bowl and pour over hot bread in pans. Let cool before removing from pans.
