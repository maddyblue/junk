FROZEN FRUIT SLUSHES
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 2 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 1/2 Cups ice cubes 1 banana, sliced 5 strawberries, hulled
1/2 Cup orange juice
Mix all ingredients in blender until thick and smooth. Serve immediately.
