FRENCH DIP
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Sandwiches
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Pounds chuck roast, trimmed 2 Cups water
1/2 Cup soy sauce 1 Teaspoon rosemary, dried 1 Teaspoon thyme, dried 1 Teaspoon garlic powder 1 bay leaf 3 peppercorns, whole 8 french rolls, split
Place roast in a slow cooker. Add water, soy sauce and seasonings. Cover and cook on high for 5-6 hours or until beef is tender. Remove meat from broth; shred with forks and keep warm. Strain broth; skim off fat. Pour broth into small cups for dipping. Serve beef on rolls.
