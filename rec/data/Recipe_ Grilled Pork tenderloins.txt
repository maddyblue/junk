GRILLED PORK TENDERLOINS
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Pork Chops
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Tablespoon orange peel, grated
1/2 Cup orange juice 1/4 Cup chicken broth 1 Teaspoon tarragon leaves, dried 1 Tablespoon soy sauce 2 pork tenderloin, 3/4 lb
Mix all ingredients and put in pork. Cover with plastic wrap. Microwave on high 5 minutes. Cover and grill 20 minutes with medium coals, turning several times until no longer pink.
