ORANGE SALAD WITH HONEY DRESSING
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1/4 Cup sugar 8 Teaspoons lemon juice 2 Tablespoons vinegar 5 Teaspoons honey
1/2 Teaspoon mustard, ground 1/2 Teaspoon paprika 1/8 Teaspoon salt 1/8 Teaspoon celery seed 1/2 Cup vegetable oil
Boston Lettuce 6 oranges, peeled and sliced
In a blender or food processor, combine the first nine ingredients; blend until slightly thickened. Pour into a jar or airtight container; chill. Line a serving platter or individual salad plates with lettuce. Arrange orange slices on top. Shake dressing; drizzle over oranges.
