SPINACH NOODLE CASSEROLE
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Casseroles
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
20 Ounces frozen spinach, chopped, thawed
1 Pint sour cream 1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 Teaspoon salt 1/2 Teaspoon pepper 2 Tablespoons parmesan cheese 6 Ounces swiss cheese, sliced
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Squeeze water from thawed spinach.
Set aside. Mix sour cream, soup, salt, pepper, and parmesan cheese in mixing bowl. In a deep dish spread one heaping teaspoon of sour cream. Alternate layers of noodles, spinach, sour cream and swiss cheese.
Repeat. top with thin layer of sour cream. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake uncovered for 1 hour.
