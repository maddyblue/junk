________________

SOPAIPILLIAS
Recipe By : Joy of Cooking Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 package yeast, dry, active 1 Cup water, warm 1 1/2 Cups flour 1 Teaspoon salt 1 Tablespoon shortening 1 1/2 Cups flour
Combine yeast and warm water. Let stand 3-5 minutes. Add and beat until smooth 1 1/2 cups four, salt, and shortening. Add 1 1/2 cups more flour. Place in a greased bowl, cover and let rise until doubled in bulk, about 45 minutes.
Roll out and cut into 3 inch squares and deepfat fry at 365 degrees. Season with cinnamon and sugar
