HOLIDAY EGGNOG
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Quart milk 10 eggs
2 Cups sugar 2 Tablespoons rum flavoring 2 Cups half and half 1 Quart vanilla ice cream, softened
Beat egg yolks. Set aside. Whip egg whites until stiff. Set aside. In large punch bowl add milk, sugar, rum flavoring, half and half and ice cream. Stir until ice cream has melted. Add egg yolks. Fold in egg whites. Makes 4 quarts.
