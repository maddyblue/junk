ARTICHOKE DIP
Recipe By : Susan henderson Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Appetiziers
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 can artichokes, not marinated 1 Cup mayonnaise 1 Cup parmeasen cheese
Chop up artichokes. mix with all other ingredients.
Bake 400 degrees for 10 minutes.
Serve hot with triskets.
