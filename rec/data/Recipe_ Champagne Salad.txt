CHAMPAGNE SALAD
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
8 Ounces cream cheese
3/4 Cup sugar 2 Cups strawberries, fresh 1 can pineapple, crushed 2 bananas, diced 1 Cup nuts, chopped 10 Ounces cool whip
Cream sugar and cheese; add strawberries, drained pineapple, bananas and nuts. Fold in cool whip. Freeze in individual molds or glassware or baked pie shells. Put out to thaw 1/2 hour before serving.
