GERMAN HOT NOODLE SALAD
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Pasta Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Cups wide egg noodles 3 strips bacon
1/4 Cup onion, chopped 1 Tablespoon sugar 1 Tablespoon flour
1/4 Teaspoon salt 1/8 Teaspoon mustard, ground 1/2 Cup water 1/4 Cup cider vinegar 1 Cup celery, sliced 2 Tablespoons parsley, fresh, chopped
Cook noodles according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a skillet, cook the bacon until crisp. Crumble and set aside. Reseve 1 tablespoon drippings in the skillet; saute onion until tender. Stir in sugar, flour, salt and mustard; add water and vinegar . Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly, about 2-3 minutes. Rinse and drain noodles; add to skillet. Stir in celery and parsley; heat through. Transfer to a serving bowl; sprinkle with bacon.
