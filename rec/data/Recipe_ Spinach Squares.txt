SPINACH SQUARES
Recipe By : Serving Size : 92 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Appetiziers
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
20 Ounces spinach, frzen, chooped
3 Tablespoons butter 1 small onion, chopped
1/4 Pound mushrooms, sliced 4 eggs
1/4 Cup bread crumbs 1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 Pound Parmesan Cheese,Grated 1/8 Teaspoon pepper 1/8 Teaspoon basil 1/8 Teaspoon oregano leaves
Place spinach in a wire strainer under hot water to thaw, then press out all the water; set aside. melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat; add onion and mushrooms and cook , stirring until onion is limp.
In a bowl, beat eggs with a fork; then stir in bread crumbs, mushroom soup, 2 tablespoon of the cheese, pepper, basil, oregano, drained spinach and onion mixture til blended.
Turn into a well greased 9 inch square pan. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake uncovered at 325 degresse for 35 minutes or until set. Cool slightly, then cover and refrigerate.
Cut into 1 inch squares and serve cold or reheat at 325 degrees for 10-12 minutes.
