FRESH CORN CHOWDER
Recipe By : Denver Post Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Soups
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
4 Cups corn, cut from cob 1 Tablespoon margarine 1 onion, chopped 1 Cup celery, diced 2 Ounces ham, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 1/2 Cups potatoes, peeled and diced 2 cans chicken broth = 20 ounces
1/4 Cup flour ` 2 Tablespoons flour
1/2 Teaspoon salt 1/4 Teaspoon pepper 1/8 Teaspoon cayenne pepper 2 Cups milk 1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
Shredded cheddar cheese
In blender, process 2 1/2 cups corn until smooth. Melt maragine in Dutch oven. Add onion, celery, ham and garlic. Saute until celery is soft, stirring occasionally. Add potatoes and broth. Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, about 15 minutes or until potatoes are almost tender. Stir frequently. Add corn puree and remaining kernels. Cook, uncovered 10 minutes over moderate heat.
in a small bowl, mix together flour, salt, black pepper and cayenne. Gradually add milk and worcestershire, whisking until smooth. Add to chowder. Cook over moderate heat, stirring frequently, about 10 minutes or until thickened.
