FRUIT FROZEN PUNCH
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Cups water 2 Cups sugar 3 bananas, mashed 1 lemon, juiced 3 oranges,juiced 1/2 large can pineapple juice
Mix all ingredients together. Freeze. Serve with 7 up or lemon lime.
