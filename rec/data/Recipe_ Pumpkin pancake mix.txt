PUMPKIN PANCAKE MIX
Recipe By : William Sonoma Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breakfast
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
4 Cups pumkin pancake mix 1 1/2 egg 1 1/2 Tablespoons butter, melted 1 1/2 Cups Milk,Lowfat (2%), Milk Solids Added
In a large bowl whisk together the mix, egg, butter and milks until the batter is smooth and no lumps remain; do not overbeat. Let stand for 5 minutes.
