ROAST GLAZED CHICKEN
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Pounds whole chicken
1/2 Cup frozen apple juice concentrate, thawed 1 Teaspoon cornstarch
1/8 Teaspoon cinnamon 2 lemon slices
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Remove skin and visible fat from breast portion of chicken.
Combine apple juice concentrate, cornstarch and cinnamon in small saucepan, mixing well to blend in cornstarch. Add lemon slices. Cook over medium heat until thickened, stirring constantly. Remove lemon slices and spoon part fo glaze over chicken breast. Roast for 1 1/4 - 1 1/2 hours, basting breast with glaze every 20 minutes. During last basting, bast entire chicken with glaze. If chicken browns to quickly, cover with foil tent.
