CREAMY POTATO STICKS
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Vegetables And Side Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1/4 Cup flour 1/2 Teaspoon salt 1 1/2 Cups milk 1 can cream of celery soup, undiluted
1/2 Pound american cheese, cubed 5 large baking potatoes, peeled 1 Cup onion, chopped
In a saucepan, combine flour and salt; gradually whisk in milk until smooth. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat; whisk in soup and cheese until smooth. Set aside. Cut potatoes into 4-inch sticks; place in a greased 13-in by 9 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with onion. Top with cheese sauce. Bake, un covered, at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes or until pottatoes are tender. Sprinkle with paprika.
