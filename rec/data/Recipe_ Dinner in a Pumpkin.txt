DINNER IN A PUMPKIN
Recipe By : Pam Ho Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
5 pund pumpkin 1 Pound ground beef 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 onion, chopped 1 Cup rice, uncooked 4 Tablespoons butter 1 Teaspoon salt
1/2 Teaspoon pepper 1 Teaspoon thyme 1 can tomatoes 2 Cups beef broth
1/2 Cup cheddar cheese, grated
Wash pumpkin and cut off the top, creatin a lid. Scoop out seeds and stringy fibers. Brush the inside of pumpkin with melkted butter and salt.
In a skillet, saute ground beef, onion, garlic and rice in butter until browned. breaking up meat with a fork. Add remaining ingredients except cheese and bring to a boil. Stuff pumpkin with this mixture, replace the lid. Place the pumpkin in a shallow baking pan and bake at 350 degrees for about 1 1/2 hours or until the pumpkin flesh inside is tender. Uncover, and sprinkle inside with cheese and return to oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serve from the pumpkin, scraping some of the pumpkin pulp with each serving.
