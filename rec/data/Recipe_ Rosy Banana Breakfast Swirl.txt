ROSY BANANA BREAKFAST SWIRL
Recipe By : Denver Post Serving Size : 2 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breakfast
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 bananas
3/4 Cup raspberry juice 3/4 Cup low fat buttermilk 1/2 Teaspoon vanilla 2 ice cubes
Cut bananas in thirds. Place in blender with remaining ingredients. Blend until smooth. Serves 2.
