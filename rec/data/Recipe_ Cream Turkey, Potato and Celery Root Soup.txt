CREAM TURKEY, POTATO AND CELERY ROOT SOUP
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Soups
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
4 Cups Turkey Stock or chicken broth 1 Pound potatoes, peeled cut in 1 in. pieces 1 Pound celery, root, peeld cut in 1 in. pieces 6 large garlic cloves, peeled 1 large carrot, peeled 1 bay leaf
1/2 Teaspoon thyme, dried 1 Cup milk 1 Cup turkey, cooked, shredded
Combine first 7 ingredients in heavy large saucepan and bring to b oil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer until vegetables are very tender, about 30 minutes. Uncover and cool slightly. Remove carrot and dice finely; set aside. Discard bay leaf.
Using slotted spoon, transfer remaining vegetables to processor; puree until smooth. Add liquid from saucepan; blend until smooth. Return soup to same saucepan. Stir in milk, turkey and diced carrot; bring to simmer. Season with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls and serve.
