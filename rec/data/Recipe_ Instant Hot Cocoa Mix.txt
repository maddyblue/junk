INSTANT HOT COCOA MIX
Recipe By : Denise Wood Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
20 Quarts powdered milk
can Hershey's instant 1 large jar cremora-powdered creamer 2 1/2 Cups powdered sugar 2 t salt, if desired
Mix well all ingredients.
Use 5 teaspoons per mug.
