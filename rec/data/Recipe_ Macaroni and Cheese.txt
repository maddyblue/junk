MACARONI AND CHEESE
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Vegetables And Side Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Cup sour cream 1 Cup cottage cheese 1 Cup cheddar cheese 3 Tablespoons onions 1 pkg egg noodles
Cook noodles as directed.
Mix sour cream, cottage cheese, and cheddar cheese. Mix with noodles. Put in casserole dish. Bake 350 degress for 30 minutes.
