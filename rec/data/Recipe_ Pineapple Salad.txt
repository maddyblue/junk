PINEAPPLE SALAD
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Pound pineapple, crushed, canned 1 pkg gelatin, unflavored 1 Cup sugar 1/2 Cup water 1 Cup cheddar cheese, grated 1 Cup cream, whipped
Soften gelatin in water. Drain pineapple juice to make. Add water to juice to amek 1 1/2 cu liquid. Add sugar to liquid and bring to boil. Add gelation. Refrigerate until almost set.
Mix whipped cream cheddar cheese and pineapple together. Add to gelatin mixture. Set over night.
