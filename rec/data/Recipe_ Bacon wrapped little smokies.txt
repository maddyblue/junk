BACON WRAPPED LITTLE SMOKIES.
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Appetiziers
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
little smokies bacon brown sugar
Cut bacon slices in half. Wrap little smokies in bacon, attached with a toothpick.
Spray a cookie sheet. Place little smokies on cookie sheet. Sprinkle with brown sugar.
Bake 350-375 degrees 10-15 minutes
