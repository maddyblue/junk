GRAVY
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Turkey
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Tablespoons drippings 2 Tablespoons flour 1 Cup juices
To drippings add flour. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly. Stir in liquid. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
