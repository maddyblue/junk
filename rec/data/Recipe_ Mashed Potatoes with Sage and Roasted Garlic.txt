MASHED POTATOES WITH SAGE AND ROASTED GARLIC
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Vegetables And Side Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Pounds russet potatoes, peeled, cut
1/2 Cup milk 3 Tablespoons butter 2 Teaspoons sage, dried, rubbed 2 Tablespoons roasted garlic puree(from chicken)
Place potatoes in large saucepan. Cover with water. Boil until tender. Drain well. Return to pot and mash with 1/2 cup milk, butter and sage. Season with salt and pepper. Mix in 2 tablespoons garlic puree. Thin with additional milk, if desired.
