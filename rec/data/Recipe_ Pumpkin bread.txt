PUMPKIN BREAD
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories :
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Cups sugar 1 Cup oil 4 eggs, beaten 2 Cups pumpkin 3 1/2 Cups flour 1 Teaspoon baking powder 1 Teaspoon cinnamon 1 Teaspoon nutmeg 1 Teaspoon cloves 1 Teaspoon allspice 2/3 Cup water 1/2 Cup nuts 1 Cup raisins
Combine sugar, oil and eggs. Add pumpkin. Sift togehter dry ingredients; add to pumpkin. Add water. Fold in nuts.
Pour into 4 greased small loaf pans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes.
