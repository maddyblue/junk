HEARTY NEW ENGLAND DINNER
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 medium carrots, sliced 1 medium onion, sliced 1 celery, sliced 3 Pounds chuch roast, boneless 1 Teaspoon salt
1/4 Teaspoon pepper 1 envelope onion soup mix 2 Cups water
1/2 head cabbage, cut in wedges 3 Tablespoons butter 2 Tablespoons flour 1 Tablespoon onion, dried, minced 2 Tablespoons horseradish
Place carrots, onion and celery in a 5-quart slow cooker. Place the roast on top; sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper. Add soup mix, water, vinegar and bay leaf. Cover and cook on low for 7-9 hours or until beef is tender. Remove beef and keep warm; discard bay leaf. Add cabbage. Cover and cook on high for 30 minutes or until cabbage is tender.
Meanwhile, melt butter in a small saucepan; stir in flour and onion. Add 1 1/2 cups cooking liquid from the slow cooker. Stir in horseradish and remaining salt; bring to boil. Cook and stir over low heat until thick and smooth, about 2 minutes. Serve with roast and vegetables.
