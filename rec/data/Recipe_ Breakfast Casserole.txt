BREAKFAST CASSEROLE
Recipe By : Lori keating Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breakfast
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
8 slices bread 1 Pound sausage or bacon 1 Pound cheddar cheese, mild 8 eggs 4 Cups milk 2 Teaspoons salt
Dash worcestershire sauce
Remove crusts from bread and cut in cubes. Line pan with bread. Brown sausage, and crumble and put on bread. Sprinkle on cheddar cheese.
Mix well eggs, milk , salt and worcestershire sauce. pour on top. Refrigerate overnight. Bake 350 degrees for 1 hour.
