DINNER IN A DISH
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 12 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes Casseroles
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Pounds ground beef 1 medium onion, chopped 3 Cups peas, frozen
2/3 Cup ketchup 1/4 Cup parsley, fresh, chopped 2 Tablespoons flour 2 Teaspoons beef bouillon granules 2 Teaspoons marjoram, dried 1 Teaspoon salt
1/2 Teaspoon pepper 6 Cups mashed potatoes, hot 2 eggs
In a saucepan over medium heat, brown the beef and onion; drain. Add the next nine ingredients; mix well. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Pour into an ungreased shallow 3-quart baking dish. Combine potatoes and eggs; mix well. Drop by 1/2 cupfuls onto beef mixture. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until bubbly and potatoes are light browned.
