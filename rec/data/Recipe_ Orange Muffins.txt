ORANGE MUFFINS
Recipe By : Colorado Cache Serving Size : 36 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Cup sugar 1 orange, juiced
1/2 Cup butter 1 Cup sugar 3 3/8 Cups sour cream 2 Cups flour, sifted 1 Teaspoon baking soda 1 Teaspoon salt 1 Teaspoon orange rind, grated
1/2 Cup raisins 1/2 Cup nuts, chopped
Mix sugar and orange juice. Set aside for dipping after muffins are cooked. Cream butter and sugar. Add sour cream alternately with the dry ingredients. Fold in orange rind, raisins and nuts. This is a stiff batter. Use well greased muffin tins that are small so that these are bite-sized. Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes. While still warm, dip them in the sugar-orange juice mixture. Cool on a wire rack.
