SLOPPY JOES
Recipe By : Marie Polleck Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
4 Pounds hamburger 2 onions, chopped 2 Cups celery, chopped
1/8 Cup lemon juice 2 1/2 Cups ketchup
1/2 Cup tomato juice 1/4 Cup brown sugar 3/4 Teaspoon salt 3/4 Teaspoon dry mustard
Brown meat with onions and celery. Drain. Add other ingredeints. Heat through.
