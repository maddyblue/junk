BOW TIE PASTA SALAD
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Cup vegetable Oil
2/3 Cup white wine vinegar 2/3 Cup Teriyaki Sauce 6 Tablespoons sugar 1/2 Teaspoon salt 1/2 Teaspoon pepper 16 Ounces bow tie pasta 10 Ounces spinach craisins 3 cans manderin oranges 16 Ounces waterchestnuts, sliced
1/2 Cup parsley, chopped 1 bunch green onions
honey roasted peanuts sesame seeds
Cook Bow Tie pasta drain and rinse with cold water.
Mix first 6 ingredients together for dress and pour over bow tie pasta. Marinate for at least 2 hours.
Add all other ingredients and serve
