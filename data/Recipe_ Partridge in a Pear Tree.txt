PARTRIDGE IN A PEAR TREE
Recipe By : Serving Size : 10 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 can pear halves, drained, 16 0z 10 maraschino cherries without stem
2 small Lime flavored gelatin 2 Cups boiling water 1 Cup water, cold 1 Cup cool whip, thawed
Cut 2 or 3 pear halves into 8 slices; dice remaining pears. Place slices and cherries on bottom of tree mold.
Dissolve 1 package gelatin in 1 cup boiling water. Add 1/2 cup cold water; chill until thickened. Carefully pour over fruit in mold; push fruit down if it floats; chill until set but not firm.
Dissolve remaining package of gelatin in remaining boiling water. Add remaining cold water; chill until slightly thickened. Stir in whipped topping and diced pears. Spoon over gelatin in mold; chill until firm, aboutt 4 hours. Unmold. Garnish with candy canes, if desired.
