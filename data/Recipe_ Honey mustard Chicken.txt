HONEY MUSTARD CHICKEN
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 1/2 Pounds chicken parts
1/3 Cup honey 1/4 Cup Dijon mustard
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Remove skin and all visible fat from chicken parts. Place in baking dish. Combine honey and mustard in bowl. pour oven chicken and turn pieces meat side down. Bake 25 minutes. Turn chicken and bake 15-20 minutes longer, basting every 10 minutes until meat is tender and juices run yellow, not pink.
