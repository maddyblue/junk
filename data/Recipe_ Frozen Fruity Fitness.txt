FROZEN FRUITY FITNESS
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 2 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 1/2 Cups ice, crushed 6 Tablespoons orange juice 6 Tablespoons pineapple juice 6 Tablespoons white grape juice 6 Tablespoons cranberry juice cocktail 6 Tablespoons apple juice 1 banana, cut into pieces
Mix all ingredients in blender until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
