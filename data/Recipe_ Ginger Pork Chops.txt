GINGER PORK CHOPS
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Pork Chops
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1/3 Cup apple juice 2 Tablespoons soy sauce 1 Tablespoon ginger root, finely chopped 1 large garlic clove, chopped 4 pork chops
Mix all ingredients except pork chops in shallow dish. Place pork chops in dish; turn to coat with marinade. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Cover and grill 14-16 minutes brushing with marinade.
