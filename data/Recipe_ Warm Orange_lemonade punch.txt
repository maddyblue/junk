WARM ORANGE/LEMONADE PUNCH
Recipe By : Denise Serving Size : 10 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
12 Ounces orange juice concentrate 12 Ounces lemonade concentrate 20 Cups water
2 Teaspoons almond extract 2 Teaspoons vanilla extract 1 1/2 Cups sugar
Heat everything in big pot. Serve warm.
