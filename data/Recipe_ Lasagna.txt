LASAGNA
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Pound ground beef 1 Can sausage 1 can tomato paste, small 16 Ounces tomato sauce
1 clove garlic 1 Teaspoon basil 1 Teaspoon oregano 2 Teaspoons salt 3 Cups cottage cheese 2 eggs, beaten 2 Teaspoons parsley
1/2 Cup parmesan cheese 10 pkg wide egg noodles 16 Ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
Brown meat. Add tomato paste, tomato sauce, garlic, basil oregano, and salt. Simmer 1 hour
Combine cottage cheese, eggs, parsley, and parmedan cheese.
Cook egg noodle as directed.
In 9 by 13 pan layer noodles, cheese mixture, meat and mozzerala chees. Repeat.
cook 350 degreess for 30-45 minutes.
