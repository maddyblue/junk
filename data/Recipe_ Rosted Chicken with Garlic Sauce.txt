ROSTED CHICKEN WITH GARLIC SAUCE
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 4 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
7 Pounds roasting chicken
olive oil 2 1/4 Teaspoons sage, dried, rubbed 2 garlic heads, separated into cloves, unpeeled 3 Tablespoons vinegar
3/4 Cup chicken broth 2 Tablespoons chicken broth 2 Tablespoons flour
Preheat oven to 450. Pat chicken dry. Rub inside and out with oil and 2 teaspoons sage. Season generously inside and out with salt and pepper. Tie legs together to hold shape. Place in roasting pan. Surround chicken with garlic; drizzle oil over garlic. Roast 15 minutes. Remove garlic. Reduce oven temperature to 375 and roast chicken until juices run clear when thickest part of thigh is pierced, about 1 hour 10 minutes longer. Transfer chicken to platter; tent with foil.
Squeeze garlic between fingertips to release cloves. Place garlic in bowl. Mash with fork. Pour pan juices into glass measuring cup; degrease. Add vinegar to roasting pan; bring to boil over medium-high heat, scraping up any browned bits. Add to measuring cup along with any drippings from chicken. Add enough broth to measure 1 cup. Pour broth into heavy small saucepan. Stir 2 tablespoons broth and flour in small bowl until smooth. Add to saucepan. Mix in 1 teaspoon garlic puree and remaining 1/4 teaspoon sage. Reserve remaining garlic for Mashed Potatoes with Sage and Roasted Garlic. bring gravy to boil, whisking constantly. Boil until reduced to sauce consistancy, stirring frequently, about 5 minutes. Serve with chicken.
