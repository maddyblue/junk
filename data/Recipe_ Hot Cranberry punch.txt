HOT CRANBERRY PUNCH
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Tablespoons allspice 2 sticks cinnamon 1 Pint cranberry juice
1/2 Cup water 1/3 Cup packed brown sugar 2 Teaspoons cloves, whole 2 1/2 Cups pineapple juice
Make in a 10-12 cup electric percolator. put spices in basket. pour liquid into reservoir. Percolate until hot.
