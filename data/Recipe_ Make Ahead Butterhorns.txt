MAKE AHEAD BUTTERHORNS
Recipe By : Denise Wood Serving Size : 48 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Tablespoons yeast
1/3 Cup warm water 9 Cups flour 2 Cups milk, warm 1 Cup shortening 1 Cup sugar 6 eggs 2 Teaspoons salt 4 Tablespoons butter, melted
Dissolve yeast in warm water.
In mixing bowl put in 4 cups of flour, milk, shortening, sugar, eggs, and salt. Add yeast and beat until smooth. Add remaining flour until enough. Kneed.
Rise until doubled.
Punch down and divide into 4 parts. Roll into 9 inch circle and brush with butter. Cut each circle into 12 wedges. Roll up wedges and put on cookie sheet until frozen. When frozen put into ziplock bags.
To cook: Take out frozen rolls. Put on greased baking sheet and let thaw for 5 hours. Bake 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.
