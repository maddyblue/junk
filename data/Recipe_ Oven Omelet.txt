OVEN OMELET
Recipe By : Linda jibson Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breakfast
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1/2 Cup butter 18 eggs
1 Cup sour cream 1 Cup milk 2 Teaspoons salt
1/4 Cup green onion, chopped
In oven melt butter in a 9 by 13 baking dish. tilt dish to coat bottom with butter. mix all ingredients and pour in pan. Bake 325 degrees until eggs are set but moist about 45 minutes.
