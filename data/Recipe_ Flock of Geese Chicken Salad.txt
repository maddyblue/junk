FLOCK OF GEESE CHICKEN SALAD
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 12 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
8 chicken breast halves, boneless cooked, cubed 2 Cups red grapes, seedless, halved 2 Cups cashew halves, salted 2 celery ribs, sliced
3/4 Cup mayonnaise 1/2 Cup sour cream 1 Tablespoon white wine vinegar
In a large bowl, combine the first four ingredients; set aside.
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream and vinegar; mix well. Pour over chicken mixture; toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Serve in a lettuce-lined bowl if desired.
