PEPPERED BEEF TENDERLOIN
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Teaspoon oregano, dried 1 Teaspoon paprika 1 Teaspoon thyme, dried 1 Teaspoon salt
1/2 Teaspoon garlic powder 1/2 Teaspoon onion powder 1/2 Teaspoon pepper 1/2 Teaspoon pepper, while 1/8 Teaspoon cayenne pepper 3 Pounds beef tenderloin
Combine seasonings and rub overf entire tenderloin. Place on a rack in a roasting pan. Bake, uncovered at 425 degrees until meat is cooked as desired. allow approximately 45-50 minutes for rare or until a meat thermometer reads 140 degress, 55-60 minutes for medium-rare(150), 62-65 imutes for medium (160) and 67-70 minutes for well-done. Let stand 10 minutes before carving.
Note: After seasoning, the uncooked tenderloin may be wrapped tightly and refrigerated over night for a more intesne flavor.
