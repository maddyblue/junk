ORANGE CREAM FRUIT SALAD
Recipe By : Taste of Home Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
20 Ounces pineapple tidbits \ 16 Ounces peach slices, drained 11 Ounces manderin oranges, drained
2 medium bananas, sliced 1 medium apple, chopped 1 pkg vanilla pudding mix, small 1 1/2 Cups milk
1/3 Cup frozen orange juice 3/4 Cup sour cream
In a large salad bowl, combine fruits; set aside. In a small mixing bowl, beat pudding mix, milk and orange juice concentrate for 2 minutes. Add sour cream; mix well. Spoon over fruit; toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.
