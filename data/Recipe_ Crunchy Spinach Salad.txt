CRUNCHY SPINACH SALAD
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Quarts spinach, torn 2 Cups bean sprouts, fresh 8 Ounces water chestnuts, sliced 4 eggs, hard cooked, chopped 6 bacon strips, crumbled 1 onion, sliced
DRESSING 1/2 Cup brown sugar 1/2 Cup vegetable oil 1/3 Cup vinegar 1/3 Cup ketchup 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
Combine spinach, sprouts, chestnuts, eggs, bacon and onion.
In bottle combine all dressing ingredients. Cover and shake well to mix. Just beforeserving pour over salad and toss.
