FRESH SPINACH PASTA
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Pasta
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 3/4 Cups flour, bread
2/3 Cup spinach, finely chopped 1/2 Cup flour, semolina 1 large egg 3 Tablespoons water
bread flour
Blend first 4 ingredients in processor. Gradually mix in enough water by tablespoons to form dough. Gather dough into ball; flatten into disk. Wrap dough in plastic and let stand 30 minutes at room temperature.
Cut dough into 6 pieces. Flatten 1 piece (keep remainder wrapped) and dust with bread flour. Turn pasta machine to widest setting and run dough through twice. Adjust machine to narrower setting and roll dough through until pasta is 1/16 inch thick. Cut pasta into 2-inch squares. Repeat with remaining dough pieces. (Can be made 1 day ahead. Arrange in single layers between sheets of waxed paper on baking sheet. Cover; chill.)
