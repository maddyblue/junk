WAKEFIELD SPICE CIDER
Recipe By : Pat Rembold Serving Size : 24 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Gallon apple cider 1 Tablespoon whole cloves 1 Tablespoon allspice 1 stick cinnamon 1 Cup sugar
Place spices in cheesecloth bag. Bring cider, spices and sugar to boil. Simmer 10 minutes and remove bag of spices. keep hot but not boiling.
This is served on George Washington's birthday at Wakefield, his birthplace.
