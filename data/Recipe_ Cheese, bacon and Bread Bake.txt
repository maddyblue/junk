CHEESE, BACON AND BREAD BAKE
Recipe By : Denver Post Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breakfast
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 1/2 Cups flour 1 1/2 Teaspoons baking powder 1/2 Teaspoon baking soda 1/2 Teaspoon dillweed, dried 1/2 Teaspoon lemon peel, finel shredded 8 Ounces sour cream 8 slices bacon
1/2 Cup ricotta cheese 1/2 Cup swiss cheese, shredded 3 Ounces cream cheese 3 Tablespoons milk 2 Teaspoons mustard, dijon 1 green onion, finely chopped 1 Tablespoon parsely, snipped
In a medium bowl stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda, dillweed and lemon peel. Stir in sour cream. On a lightly floured surface, knead dough until smooth. Cover and set aside.
In a 10-inch oven safe skillet, cook bacon until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Crumble when cool enough to handle. Set aside. Cool skillet; wipe with paper towels. Press dough onto the bottom and 1/2inch up the sides of the skillet; sprinkle with all but 2 tablesppons of the crumbled bacon. In a medium mixking bowl stir together ricotta, swiss chesse, cream cheese, milk, mustard, green onion and parsle; spread in the skillet.
Bake in a 400 degree over about 25 minutes or until golden. Cool slightly. Cut into wedges to serve. Sprinkle with reserved bacon
