CHEESE AND SHELL BAKE
Recipe By : Karlena Gonzalez Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Pound ground beef
1/2 Cup onion, chopped 2 Cups tomato sauce 1 Teaspoon garlic salt 1/4 t pepper 1 1/4 Cups cheddar cheese, shredded 1 Cup sour cream 1 Teaspoon parsley 4 Cups small shells, 2 cups uncooked
Brown beef- add onion and saute. Drain fat.
Stir in tomato sauce, garlic salt and pepper. Simmer. cook shells.
Combine cheese, sour cream and parsley.
Pour 1/2 of meat sauce into casserole dish that is lightly greased. Stir in 1/2 of shells. Spread cheese sauce evenly on top. Top with remaining shells and then meat sauce. Sprinkle some cheddar cheese on top. Cover and bake 350 degres for 25 minutes.
