HONEY ORANGE CHICKEN
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 8 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 Teaspoon salt 1/8 Teaspoon pepper 4 boneless chicken breast 2 Tablespoons butter
1/2 Cup chicken broth 2 Tablespoons orange juice 1 Teaspoon dijon mustard
1/2 Teaspoon honey
orange slices
1. in a plastic bag or small paper bag, combine the cornstarch , salt and pepper and shake to mix. Add the chicken and shake to coat the chicken lightly. Remove the chicken and reserve the excess cornstarch mixture.
2. In a large skillet, melt 1 tablespoon of the butter over medium heat until hot but not smoking. Add the chicken and brown on one side, about 5 minutes.
3. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon butter, turn the chicken and brown well on the second side, abnout 5 minutes longer. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.
$. Dissolve the reserved cornstarch mixture in the chicken broth. Whisk the broth, orange juice concentrate, mustard and honey inot the juices in the skillet. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Add the chicken, reduce the heat to medium-low and cover the skillet. Cook until the chicken is tender and opaque throughout, 5-8 minutes. Divide the chicken and sauce among 4 plates. Sprinkle with parsley and garnish with orange slices, if desired.
