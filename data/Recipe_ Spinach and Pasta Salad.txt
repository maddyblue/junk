SPINACH AND PASTA SALAD
Recipe By : Callie Carlston Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Pasta Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
16 Ounces bowtie pasta, cooked al dente 10 Ounces spinach 11 Ounces manderin oranges
1/2 Cup parsley, chopped 1/4 Cup sesame seeds, toasted 1 bunch green onions, chopped 6 Ounces honey roasted peanuts 6 Ounces craisins 2 Cups chicken, cut into small pieces-optional
DRESSING 1 Cup vegetable oil
2/3 Cup Teriyaki sauce 2/3 Cup white wine vinegar 6 Tablespoons sugar 1/2 Teaspoon salt 1/2 Teaspoon pepper
Blend dressing ingredients together in blender or food processor. MIx dressing and cooked pasta in medium bowl and marinate for 2 hours in teh refrigerator. Combine the rest of the salad ingredients in a large salad bow, add pasta/dressing and toss!
A lot of the ingredients are optional 9crasins, chicken, water chestnuts, peanuts, etc)
