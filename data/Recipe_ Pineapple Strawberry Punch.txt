PINEAPPLE STRAWBERRY PUNCH
Recipe By : Denise Wood Serving Size : 20 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 can pineapple juice, frozen 1 bag strawberries, frozen 2 Liters ginger ale
Take frozen can of juice out of freezer to soften. Put frozen juice and strawberries in bowl and mash to make slush. Add ginger ale and serve
