SOMETHING ONIONS
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories :
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
onions, sliced thin whilte bread, crusts mayo Parmesan Cheese,Grated
Cut out rounds of bread, white, no cursts.
Make mixture of 2/3 mayo to 1/3 parmesan cheese.
Spray cookie sheet with pam. Put cut bread on sheet. Place slice onions over bread. Spread mayo mixture over onion. Sprinkle with Paprika, then broil, until golden.
