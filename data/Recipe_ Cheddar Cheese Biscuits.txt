CHEDDAR CHEESE BISCUITS
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 36 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Cups sharp cheddar cheese, loosely packed 6 Tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
1/2 Teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
Dash Tabasco Sauce 1 1/4 Cups flour
Mix cheddar cheese, unsalted butter, Worcestershire sauce and Tabasco sauce in large bowl until well blended. Add flour and stir until thoroughly combined. Shape dough into 10-inch-long log. Wrap in plastic and chill at least 3 hours. (Can be prepared 2 days ahead. Keep refrigerated.)
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cut dough into 1/4-inch thick slices. Space evenly on baking sheets. Sprinkle some with poppy seeds, some with sesame seeds and some with celery seeds. Bake until edges are golden brown, about 15 minutes. Cool biscuits on baking sheets 15 minutes. Transfer to rack and cool completely.
