MEXICAN LASAGNA
Recipe By : Barb Guzman Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beef Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 Pound hamburger 1 small onion 1 pkg spaghetti sauce mix 16 Ounces tomato sauce
1 Tablespoon butter 1 pkg fine egg noodles 1 Cup sour cream 3 Ounces cream cheese
1/2 Cup cheddar cheese, shredded
Brown hamburger with onion, salt and pepper to taste. Mix spaghetti sauce mix with tomato sauce and butter. Add to meat and simmer 10 minutes.
Boil pkg of egg noodes. Drain and rinse
Mix sour cream and cream cheese together until smooth.
On bottom of buttered 9 by 13 pan spread noodles - next spread cream cheese mixture - then meat mixture. Sprinkle on cheddar cheese. Bake 350 degreese for 30 minutes
