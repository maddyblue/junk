FRUIT SALAD DRESSING
Recipe By : Myrtle Jibson Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1/2 Cup sugar 1 Tablespoon cornstarch 1 Cup water or fruit juice 1 lemon
1/2 Cup cream, whipped
Mix sugar and cornstarch. Add liquid and cook thoroughly. Take off stove and add juice of lemon. Cool; Mix with whipped cream.
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
Notes : When fruit juice is use only 2 tablespoons of sugar are needed.
