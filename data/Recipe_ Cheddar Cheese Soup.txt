CHEDDAR CHEESE SOUP
Recipe By : Bon Appetit Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Soups
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1/4 Cup butter 2 Cups onions, chopped 2 stalks celery, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 Cup flour 1/2 Teaspoon Paprika 1/4 Teaspoon cayenne pepper 4 Cups chicken broth, low salt 1 1/2 Cups half and half 2 1/2 Cups cheddar cheese, grated 1/2 Teaspoon worcestershire sauce
Dash Tabasco Sauce 1/4 Cup Parsley,Fresh,Chopped
Melt butter in heavy large saucepan over medium- high heat. Add onions, celery and garlic and saute until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes. Mix in flour, paprika and cayenne peper and stir 3 minutes. Gradually stir in broth. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer 15 minutes. Strain. Return to same saucepan. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and rerigerate.)
Add half and half to soup and bring to simmer over medium heat. Add cheese 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until cheese melts after each addition. Mix in Worcestershire sauce, hot pepper sauce and parsley. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls and serve.
