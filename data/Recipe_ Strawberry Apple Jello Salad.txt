STRAWBERRY APPLE JELLO SALAD
Recipe By : Iris Noble Serving Size : 12 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
9 Ounces Strawberry Jello
1/3 Cup sugar 3 Cups water, boiling 1 1/2 Cups orange juice 6 small apples, peeled and shredded
In bowl put in jello and sugar. Add water and stir until disolved. Add orange juice and shredded apple. Mix well and put in 9 by 13 pan.
