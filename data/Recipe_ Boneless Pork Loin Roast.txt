BONELESS PORK LOIN ROAST
Recipe By : Safeway Serving Size : 12 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Pork Chops
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
4 Pounds pork loin roast 2 Teaspoons cornstarch
1/4 Teaspoon cinnamon 1/8 Teaspoon salt 1/2 orange peel, grated 2 Tablespoons orange juice 2 Tablespoons dry sherry 1 can whole berry cranberry sauce
In a small saucepan, stir together all ingredients except pork. Cook stirring over medium heat until thickened; set aside. Place roast in shallow baking dish. Roast at 325 degree for 45 minutes. Spoon 1/2 cup of glaze over roast and continue roasting for 30-45 minutes. Spoon 1/2 cup glaze over roast and continue roasting for 30-45 minutes more, until internal temperature is 155-160 degrees. Let stand 10 minutes before slicing and serve with remaining sauce.
