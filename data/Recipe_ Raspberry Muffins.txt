RASPBERRY MUFFINS
Recipe By : Creme de Colorado Serving Size : 12 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Breads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 1/2 Cups flour
1/2 Teaspoon baking soda 1/2 Teaspoon salt 1 1/2 Teaspoons Cinnamon,Ground 1 Cup sugar 12 Ounces raspberries, frozen, unsweetened, thaw
2 eggs, well beaten
2/3 Cup vegetable oil 1/2 Cup pecans, chopped
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In medium bow, mix flour, soda, salt, cinnamon and sugar. Make a well in the center and stir in undrained raspberries and eggs. Thoroughly mix in oil and pecans. Spoon batter into lightly greased muffin tins. muffin cups will be full. Batter is heavy and will not overflow. Bake 15-20 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before removing from pan.
This recipe can also be bake in a greased and floured 9 x 5 loaf pan at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until a wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
