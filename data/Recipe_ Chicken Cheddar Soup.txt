CHICKEN CHEDDAR SOUP
Recipe By : Nancy Pickup Serving Size : 10 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Soups
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Cups chicken broth 2 carrots, shredded 1 Cup chicken pieces 1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 Teaspoon celery seed 2 Cups grated cheddar cheese
3/4 Cup onion, chopped 4 Tablespoons butter 4 Tablespoons flour 4 Cups milk
Combine chicken broth, carrots,chicken pieces, worchietier sauce and celery see. Bring to boil. Simmer for 1 hour
Saute onion and butter. Add flour and milk to make white sauce. Add cheese to melt. Add sauce to other ingredeints and heat through.
