FROZEN FRUIT PUNCH
Recipe By : Christi Bergin Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Cups water 2 Cups sugar 3 bananas, mashed 1 lemon, juiced 3 oranges, juiced 1/2 can pineapple juice.
7 up or lemon lime
boil together water and sugar until sugar is dissolved. Add mashed bananas, the lemon juice and orange juice. Mix with pineapple juice and freeze.
Serve with 7 up - about half and half.
