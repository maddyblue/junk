CHICKEN ELEGANT
Recipe By : Linda Jibson Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
6 chicken breasts 1 Cup ham, cubed 1 can cream of chicken soup 1 Cup canned artichokes, drained(not marinate 1 Cup mayonnaise
1/2 Cup chicken broth 1 Cup swiss cheese, grated 2 Tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 Teaspoon basil 1/4 Teaspoon onion flakes 1/4 Teaspoon salt 1/2 Teaspoon curry 3/4 Cup buttered bread crumbs 1/2 Cup cashew nuts
Sprinkle chicken with basil, onion flakes and salt and bake covered in oven at 375 degrees for 40 minutes. Cool and remove meat from bones. Reserve broth. Cut chicken into large pieces and place in shallow 2 quart baking dish. distribute ham and artichokes around chicken evely.(Islice artichokes inot 3rds lengthwise, they go further.) Make sauce by blending soups, broth, mayo, lemon juice, cheese and curry. Spread sauce over chicken and sprinkle evenly with bread crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes uncovered. Before serving, sprinkle with nuts.
