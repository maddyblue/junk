WHITE CHILI
Recipe By : Cooking Light Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beans Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Teaspoons olive oil 2 onions, medium, cut up 4 garlic, cloves 2 Teaspoons cumin 2 cans chicken chunks 2 cans garbanzo beans 2 cans canellini beans 2 cans corn, shoe peg 2 cans green chilis, diced 2 cans chicken broth
Monterey Jack Cheese,Shredded
Saute first five ingredients, add chicken. Add rest of ingredients and bake at 325 degrees for 2-3 hours.
Serve with monterey jack cheese
