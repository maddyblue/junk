STRAWBERRY SALAD MOLD
Recipe By : Myrtle Jibson Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1 large strawberry jello 2 Cups boiling water 2 small strawberry boxes, frozen 1 1/2 Cups crushed pineapple 2 bananas, mashed 1 Cup nuts, chopped 1 Pint sour cream
Dissolved jello in hot water. Immediately add frozen berries and stir until thawed. Add remaining ingredients, except sour cream.
Pour 1/2 into shallow dish 8 by 11. Refrigerate until set. Carefully spread with sourcream. Pour remaining jello on top and set until firm.
