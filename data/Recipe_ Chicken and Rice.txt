CHICKEN AND RICE
Recipe By : Karlena Gonzalez Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Chicken Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 chicken breasts, cooked 1 Cup rice, uncooked 1 can cream of chicken soup 1 can cream of mushroom soup 1 can milk
parsley flakes, to taste onion flakes, to taste bread crumbs paremesan cheese
Cook rice. Tear chicken breast into pieces.. mix all ingredients together. Spread into pan and sprinkle on bread crumbs and parmesan cheese. cook 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
