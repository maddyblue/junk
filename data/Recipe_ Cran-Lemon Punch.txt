CRAN-LEMON PUNCH
Recipe By : Serving Size : 1 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Drinks
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 Quarts lemonade 1 Quart cranberry juice cocktail 1 Quart gingerale or sprite
Mix lemonade from either frozen concentrate or dry mix. Latter makes clearer punch.
Add cranberry and chill. At the last minute add chilled sprite to juices. Serve ice cold, use extra punch or lemonade to make ice ring. Add e cups pineapple juice if desired, but detracts form clarity.
