MANDARIN ORANGE SALAD
Recipe By : Colorado Cache Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Salads
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
1/2 Cup almonds, sliced 3 Tablespoons sugar
1/2 head Iceberg Lettuce,Chopped 1/2 head Romaine Lettuce,Chopped 1 Cup celery, chopped 2 Whole green onions 1 11 oz can mandarin oranges
DRESSING 1/2 Teaspoon salt
Dash pepper 1/4 Cup vegetable oil 1 Tablespoon parsley, chopped 2 Tablespoons sugar 2 Tablespoons vinegar
Dash Tabasco sauce
In a small pan over medium heat, cook almonds and sugar, stirring constantly until almonds are coated and sugar dissolved. Watch carefully as they will burn easily. Cool and store in air-tight container. Mix all dressing ingredients and chill. Mix lettuces, celery and onions. Just before serving add almonds and oranges. Toss with the dressing.
