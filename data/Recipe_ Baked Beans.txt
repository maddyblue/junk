BAKED BEANS
Recipe By : Vennette Kragenbrink Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Beans
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
2 large cans pork and beans 2 Pounds Jimmy Dean sausage 1 Cup catsup 1 Cup brown sugar 2 Tablespoons prepared mustard 1 onion
Mix all ingredients together. Bake 325 degrees 1-2 hours
