BROWN RICE
Recipe By : Serving Size : 12 Preparation Time : 0:00 Categories : Vegetables And Side Dishes
Amount Measure Ingredient Preparation -------------- ------------ -------------- ----------------
3 Cups rice, brown 30 Ounces chicken broth 15 Ounces beef broth 15 Ounces water
1 Cup onion, chopped 1/2 Cup butter, cut up
Put all ingredients in a 9x 13 pan. Cover tightly with foil. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. If rice is not tender add 1/2 cup water and bake an additional 15-20 minutes.
